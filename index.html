Data Science Consultant @ IBM | College Instructor

Nickolaus White is an experienced Data Scientist and Senior Celonis Data Engineer at IBM, specializing in process mining, automation, machine learning, and AI-driven innovation within Business Operations. He leads the development of proof-of-concept solutions — from chatbot design to task mining and tariff simulations — to enhance efficiency across Accounts Payable, Accounts Receivable, and Procurement operations.

Nickolaus collaborates directly with client SMEs to connect ERP systems, transform data, and uncover actionable insights. His expertise spans on-time payments, touchless invoices and collections, DPO, SLA optimization, free-text order management, and the application of generative AI to streamline business processes.

With a strong foundation in machine learning, time-series analysis, and computational optimization, supported by academic research and practical application, Nickolaus continually expands his technical skills and innovation capabilities. His prior coding projects are available on GitHub, all further/future work is IBM confidential.

Previously, he contributed to full-stack ERP software development in the manufacturing sector, gaining deep experience in software life cycles and stakeholder collaboration. While project specifics remain confidential due to client privacy, Nickolaus consistently drives measurable impact at the intersection of business operations and advanced technology.
