My Story

It wasn’t until the end of high school that I truly became interested in computers. This passion was sparked when I took an Advanced Placement Computer Science (AP CS) course, which led me to pursue a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science with a concentration in Artificial Intelligence at Davenport University.

After completing my undergraduate degree, I was fortunate to receive an incredible opportunity to join IBM, working within their Data and Automation department. At the same time, I was accepted into my dream graduate program — a Master’s in Data Science at Michigan State University. I committed myself to working harder than ever, balancing full-time professional work with full-time graduate studies from the summer of 2022 until graduating in the spring of 2024. It was the biggest challenge I have faced so far, but also one of the greatest blessings.

Since joining IBM and working as a Data Scientist & Celonis Consultant, I have been heavily influenced by my peers to look deeper into improving business operations. Through my current project work, helping organizations analyze and optimize their business processes, I discovered a new passion. I am now strongly considering pursuing an MBA at Purdue University–West Lafayette with a specialization in Supply Chain Strategy. My goal is to continue learning, sharpening my consulting skills, and becoming a true expert — the go-to person bridging operations, technology, and innovation.
