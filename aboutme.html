My Story


I come from a small farm-town south of Michigan's capital, Lansing, MI. I grew up with one sibling, an older brother, 
who is the person I look up to the most. I spent my childhood playing sports (soccer, basketball, tennis), going to the 
skatepark, riding ATV's, and playing a lot of videogames. It wasn't until the end of highschool that I truly became 
interested in computers, this was after I took an Advanced Placement course in Computer Science (AP CS). This provoked 
me to study Computer Science at Davenport University, where I obtained my B.S. in Computer Science with a concentration 
in Artificial Intelligence (AI). Afterwards, I was given an amazing oppurtunity to join the International Business 
Machines Corporation (IBM) to work within their Data/Automation Department. At the same time, I was also accepted into 
my dream graduate level program, a Master's in Data Science at Michigan State University. I took it upon myself to work 
harder than anyone else. Since summer of 2022, I have been expanding both my education and professional experience at a 
full-time basis. This is the biggest challenge I have faced thus far, but also the largest blessing I have recieved.
